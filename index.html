THE ARMOUR

Iron Man's armor is a fictional powered exoskeleton appearing in American
comic books published by Marvel Comics and is worn by comic book superhero
Tony Stark when he assumes the identity of Iron Man. The first armor
(which in the story, was created by Stark and Ho Yinsen), was designed by
artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby, and first appeared, along with Tony Stark,
in Tales of Suspense
